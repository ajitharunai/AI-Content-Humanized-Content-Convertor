Copyright & Responsible Use

  • 1. You own your inputs and outputs. Make sure you have the right to use any source text you paste here. Do not paste confidential or third-party copyrighted material without permission.
  • 2. Do not misrepresent AI work as fully human. This tool helps you edit and refine. It does not remove your responsibility to disclose AI assistance where required by your company, clients, or platforms.
  • 3. Do not use this to bypass AI detection systems. The purpose of this tool is to improve clarity, tone, and readability – not to trick detection models or reviewers.
  • 4. Check facts and sources. AI drafts can be inaccurate. Always verify claims, data, and citations before publishing.
  • 5. Maintain your voice. Use this as a starting point and then edit manually so the final content reflects your expertise and personality.
